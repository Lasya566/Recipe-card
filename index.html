choco banana shake

Prep: 10 mins

Serves: 2

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder(or chocolate syrup)
  • 1-2 tsp sugar(optional)
  • few ice cubes
  1. slice the banana and add it to a blender.
  2. pour in milk, cocoa powder, sugar, and ice cubes.
  3. blend until smooth and frothy.
  4. Pour into two tall glasses.
  5. drizzle with chocolate syrup or cocoa powder and serve chilled.
